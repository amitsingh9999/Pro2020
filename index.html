💮 The Challenge, Our Solution

COPD affects millions globally. Traditional diagnostic methods can be subjective and time-consuming. We introduce a state-of-the-art Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) trained on extensive lung imaging data to provide fast, objective, and highly accurate screening for COPD-related changes.

🧠 How It Works: The Deep Learning Edge

1. Data Preprocessing

Standardization and normalization of high-resolution CT scans or Chest X-rays for optimal input.

2. Model Inference

Proprietary ResNet-based architecture analyzes features like air trapping and emphysema patterns.

3. Predictive Output

Delivers an instant risk score and localization heatmaps for potential disease areas.

Model Performance: Our system demonstrates superior AUC metrics compared to baseline models, validated on international clinical datasets.

🌐 Benefits for Clinicians and Patients

  • Earlier Intervention: Detect subtle signs of COPD at Stage I, enabling prompt treatment.
  • Reduced Workload: Automate the initial screening process for radiologists and pulmonologists.
  • Consistency: Eliminate human variability with a standardized, objective AI assessment.
  • Accessibility: Potential for deployment in low-resource settings via cloud-based API.
